JUDICIAL Act of 2024

Summary

The "Justice for University Decisions, Integrity, and Academic Litigation" Act, also known as the JUDICIAL Act of 2024, transfers the authority to adjudicate academic hearing board cases to the UH SGA Department of Justice.

Details

  • Short Title: JUDICIAL Act of 2024
  • Findings: Enhances transparency, accountability, and responsiveness to student concerns.
  • Definitions: Defines key terms such as Academic Hearing Board and DOJ.
  • Transfer of Authority: Details the process and responsibilities involved in transferring adjudicative authority.
  • Structure and Roles: Establishes the roles within the SGA DOJ and their responsibilities.
  • Legality and Compliance: Ensures compliance with university policies and state laws.
  • Implementation and Oversight: Establishes an oversight committee and a project management plan.

TRANSFER Act of 2024

Summary

The "Transferring Authority, Navigating Student Finances, Events, and Resources" Act, also known as the TRANSFER Act of 2024, transfers the authority to fund student organizations, oversee major campus events, and manage investments to the UH SGA.

Details

  • Short Title: TRANSFER Act of 2024
  • Findings: Consolidates student governance and financial oversight under the SGA.
  • Definitions: Defines key terms such as Student Organization, Funding, and Major Campus Events.
  • Transfer of Funding Authority: Details the process and responsibilities for funding student organizations.
  • Transfer of Event Control: Details the process and responsibilities for managing major campus events.
  • Investment Authority: Authorizes the SGA to invest in financial instruments.
  • SFAC Responsibilities: Transfers the responsibilities of the SFAC to the SGA.
  • Legality and Compliance: Ensures compliance with university policies and state laws.
  • Implementation and Oversight: Establishes an oversight committee and a project management plan.
